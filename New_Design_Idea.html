Sam Kimball

Art, Design, Web

Memory and imagination are intertwined forces at the forefront of perception; in the right environment, ordinary things have the power to evoke strong feelings of nostalgia, wonder and atmosphere. I make art that captures these feelings by blending elements of the commonplace and bizarre.

Much of my art makes reference to the past and future. I use juxtaposition to create setting, themes and mood in my work. My glitch art overlays abandoned railroads with computer glitch effects and neon colors, reimagining those overgrown industrial spaces as being further corrupted by technology and human interference. My passion for storytelling has led me to explore mediums such as video and interactive art.
