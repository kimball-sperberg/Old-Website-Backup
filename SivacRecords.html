About

When an artist goes beyond what they already know they can do, now that to me is exciting, it’s unpredictable, it’s of the moment, and it’s never quite the same twice.

When artists of this nature gather together great moments can happen.

These recordings attempt to capture these moments. The music unfolds on its own, following its own path. It is a journey. There is a life force to it, an energy, something that as unique and distinctive as each of us.

It is exhilarating, this exchange of energy. It is music with its own identity.

Guitarist and composer John Ehlis embraces this vision with each of these recordings released on Sívac Records.
