RETURN

Distorted Journey is a video installation I made for my senior Capstone project at Champlain College. Built around the narrative of a world descending into digital chaos, I collaged footage together from unlikely sources and 'glitched' them with purely analog methods. As time passes in the video, the visuals become progressively glitchier, deteriorating into a chaotic array of distorted textures and imagery.


I displayed Distorted Journey as a piece of interactive art at the Champlain College Creative Media showcase. For the installation, the video was played on a screen in a mini arcade cabinet. The audience is given control over the video's colors through a knob attached to the cabinet.
