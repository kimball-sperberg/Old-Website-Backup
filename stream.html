[],
I didn’t recognize you
Friday morning
Sitting there alone, in your cashmere hoodie
Black and white headphones I just
Stumbled down the pathway
Head engulfed in sandstone apathy
Quiet morning exile
Shattered suddenly
I,

Sat inside myself
Pondering what can I do
If I crawl inside my shirt and wait
Will I emerge into an empty room?
I don’t know what to say
They say time changes well time’s eating me alive
I'm sitting on an empty stomach with no appetite to try

Words of wile woke me up
Evicted me from my cocoon--
We shared your tater tots
I hope I didn’t come off strong because
I know girls like the chase
I heard that on TV I think
But understand me when I say that I don’t have the time or place or nerve or waist
To act aloof
For better or for worse it seems
I put it out there now you know
I’d wake up in the morning to hang out with you
